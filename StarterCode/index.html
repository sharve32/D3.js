Health vs. Income

Conclusion Comparing Health Risks and Income


Our research has found a positive correlation amongst Poverty percentages and Healthcare decline. What's more, as the population's poverty levels increase, the lack of Healthcare percentages increase as well. Data collected from all 50 states across the nation.

Note:
Postive correlation implies as the x-values move further away from the 0-intercept, the y-values also move up in the y-axis. Negative correlation notes the inverted result, as one value increases(/decreases) the other value decreases(/increases). No correlation signifies, there was no trend found.
